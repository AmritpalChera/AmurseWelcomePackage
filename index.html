Realtor Panel, The Story of Amurse

1.0   Our Purpose

Realtor Panel aims to be a decentralized space where organizations, particularly brokerages, can completely conduct their operations digitally. The workers of the organization feel as if they are at work in real life while not being physically present. Realtor Panel brings simplicity to its very best.

Realtor Panel has now transitioned into Amurse, where any user can come in and enjoy. Of course, we will provide additional benefits for organizations as we progress.

What’s a problem that the Realtor Panel is solving?

With increasing technological advancements, most work is becoming remote. With 5G technology on the verge, this trend is set to accelerate. Whereas there are many benefits to remote work, there are also many pitfalls. For one, you are stuck within a different space, lacking the ability to make new connections and interact with others within your organization.

Most current platforms implement the solution in a complex, hard-to-use setting, to provide a real-life-like 3D space. Other platforms, including Facebook, Discord, and Slack provide a good, yet primitive solution, such as a video chat or a phone call that prohibits the delivery of key emotions between the parties, which are essential in a professional environment.

How are we solving that problem?

Realtor Panel blends the traditional messaging systems, with content posts and comments, with real-time 3D interactions, where users can get together and have a chat as they would in real life. This focuses on boosting interaction between the users and promoting engagement on the platform in a simple and user-friendly manner.
